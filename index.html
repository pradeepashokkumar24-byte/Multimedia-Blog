My favorite movies which I frequently watch 😄

1. Mugulunage- To keep smiling everyday - started in my life since 2017 🙂

Mugulunage   -  The story follows Pulakeshi (played by Ganesh), a man who has been unable to cry since birth and instead responds to every situation—including heartbreak—with a smile. The film explores his life through his relationships with three different women at different stages of his life 😊

2. Mungarumale - ಪ್ರೀತಿ ಮಧುರ ತ್ಯಾಗ ಅಮರ - Feel and experience the love and sacrifice 😣

Mungarumale   -  Mungaru Male (2006) is a landmark Indian Kannada-language romantic drama that became a massive cult classic and a historic box-office success. Directed by Yogaraj Bhat, the film stars Ganesh (in his breakout role) and Pooja Gandhi, with veteran actor Anant Nag in a supporting role 😊

3. Gaalipata - Manada mugilalli mohabatth - pure form of love and a very entertaining character which I will carry each and everyday 😍

Gaalipata (ಗಾಳಿಪಟ) most commonly refers to the acclaimed 2008 Kannada romantic drama film, though it translates literally to "kite" in Kannada 😊

4. Aramane - Smile Please - Sacrificing our own love without expresseing it to them 😓

Aramane is a 2008 Kannada drama film starring Golden Star Ganesh, Anant Nag, and Roma Asrani. Directed by Nagashekar and produced by K. Manju, the movie was released on 25 April 2008 😊

5. DDLJ - Bade bade deshon mey iise choti choti baaten hoti hee rehtii hai Senorita 😍 - A romantic entertainer

Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (DDLJ), released on 20 October 1995, is a landmark Bollywood romantic drama that redefined Indian cinema. Directed by Aditya Chopra in his directorial debut, it stars Shah Rukh Khan as Raj Malhotra and Kajol as Simran Singh, whose on-screen chemistry propelled them to superstardom 😊

6. K3G - It's all about loving your parents 😌 - A romantic movie

Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham... (2001), also known as K3G, is a landmark Bollywood family drama directed by Karan Johar. It is widely celebrated for its star-studded cast, grand production values, and the famous tagline: "It's all about loving your parents"😊
